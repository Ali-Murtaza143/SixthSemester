Lab 1, Task # 04 - Header and Footer

What is Header?

A header or heading is text at the top of a page in an electronic document or hard copy. For example, in Microsoft Word, a header could be created in a document to display the page number of each page. By contrast, a footer is at the bottom of a page in an electronic document or hard copy.

What is Footer?

In general, a footer is an area at the bottom of a document page containing data common to other pages. The information in footers may include page numbers, creation dates, copyrights, or references that appears on a single page, or on all pages.
